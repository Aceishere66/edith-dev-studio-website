# Animation Guidelines

## Current Rule

Do not add Motion, Three.js, Spline, 21st.dev/Magic components, video backgrounds, or heavy scroll effects during the foundation and early premium passes.

## Allowed Now

- CSS-only hover states.
- Short opacity, transform, border, or background transitions.
- Static CSS-generated product frames.
- Reduced-motion-safe decorative surfaces.

## Later Motion Pass

Only after product truth, conversion copy, and real assets are stable:

1. Pick one high-impact section.
2. Keep the effect tied to Upscaler Studio or the studio identity.
3. Validate desktop and mobile performance.
4. Provide a `prefers-reduced-motion` path.
5. Avoid stacking multiple heavyweight scenes.

## Do Not Animate

- Legal-sensitive setup warnings.
- Price, refund, trial availability, or FFmpeg requirements in a way that makes them easy to miss.
- Critical CTAs with distracting motion.
